Future Projects

I am not finished there, here are some of the projects I am currently working on, or hope to be shortly.


A full fantasy RPG, but text based, with ASCII Art images included.




Building on what I learned from the shipwrecked game, I want to create a larger text based game in Python.

This would be set in a fanasy setting, with you playing the hero battling the forces of evil.

It would have a much larger map, more characters, and much more ASCII art.

As well as that, it would have character creation at the beginning and a level up system throughout.

I have enough knowledge of Python to start on this but I would need to add to my skillset as I go along in order to finish it.



Project Raptor Pack, a 3rd person survival horror game created in Unreal engine 4.




Moving away from text based games and into the 3D world, the plan is to create a 30min playable demo that starts with
a cutscene.

You would play as a scavenger in a world overun by dinosaurs, with their team killed by a raptor pack, you must survive
and get back to your camp.

Imagine a cross between Dino Crisis and Alien Isolation, where the raptors are fierce, clever, deadly opponents.
One where every encounter must be carefully planned, if not avoided entirely.

I have been using many, great you-tube guides in order to expand my knowledge of the blueprints system of Unreal Engine 4,

Thankfully I will not be alone in this project, and will have the skills of my brother Matt to complete most of the
visual aspects that the game will require.
