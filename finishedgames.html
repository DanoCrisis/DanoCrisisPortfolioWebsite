Finished Games

Here are some of the completed games that I have worked on.


It always starts off small, and here it is, the first game I ever coded in Python: a simple guessing game.




After learning about random numbers in Python, I wanted to test my knowledge by creating a quick game.

It uses a simple if/else statement to ask if your number is lower or higher than the random number it generated.

After three guesses it asks if it has your number correct, and wins if it does.

The game itself is very simple, but it did present a few different problems, like how to narrow the guessing field.

I enjoyed problem solving these issues in order to get a reasonably accurate guessing game.



The next game, a text based adventure on a deserted island, coded with a team of people in Python.




The next project I undertook was a text based adventure game called Shipwrecked.

It was built in python by myself and 5 others; Lucas, Reyanud, Nasra, Kamil, and Ea-Nkoy.

In it your character traverses the island, through several different areas, looking for tools and an eventual way off the
island.

There are several npc's, battle systems, ASCII images, updating maps and even different endings.

It was a challenge to combine the different elements that each of the team brought in, but the game is only improved by
having all of their inputs.
